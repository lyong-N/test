When we talk about RBAC in K8s, there are two types:
1) Role which are limited to a particular namespace 
2) ClusterRole which are for the entire ClusterRole

*** ClusterRole don't have namespace 
-------***********-------
Once you create a role you need to bind it by creating a RoleBinding for ROLES and ClusterRoleBinding for ClusterRole

*** Users are listed in the RoleBinding or ClusterRoleBinding  under subjects
